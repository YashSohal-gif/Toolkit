How editing works

What can I add?

Text boxes (click, then type in the prompt), rectangles (click and drag), and yellow highlights (click and drag over existing text) — on any page, as many as you like.

Can I remove an edit?

Use "Undo Last" to remove the most recent addition on the current page before saving.

Is my file uploaded anywhere?

No — editing happens entirely in your browser. Your PDF never leaves your device.
