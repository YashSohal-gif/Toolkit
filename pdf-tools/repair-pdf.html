How repair works

What does this actually do?

It reads the PDF as leniently as possible, then copies every page it can successfully parse into a brand-new, cleanly-structured PDF file — this fixes most "won't open" or "corrupted structure" issues.

What if a page can't be recovered?

You'll be told how many pages were successfully recovered. If some pages are too damaged to read, they'll be skipped rather than breaking the whole file.

Is my file uploaded anywhere?

No — repair happens entirely in your browser. Your PDF never leaves your device.
