How it works

What actually happens to my notes?

Nothing is summarized, reworded, or removed. Each original page is shrunk down and placed into a grid on a new page — like the "N pages per sheet" option in a print dialog, except you get a ready-to-print PDF file with clean margins.

How many pages will 12-per-sheet save me?

A 50-page PDF becomes about 5 printed sheets at 12-per-sheet. Text gets noticeably smaller, so use 4 or 6 per sheet if you want it easier to read, or 9-12 if you mainly need it for revision/reference.

Is my file uploaded anywhere?

No — everything happens locally in your browser. Your PDF never leaves your device.
