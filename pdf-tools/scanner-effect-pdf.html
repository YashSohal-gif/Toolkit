About this effect

Why would I want this?

Sometimes a document needs to look like a physical original was scanned — a slight paper texture, a touch of imperfect alignment, and gentle shadowing at the edges.

Does this affect readability?

No — text stays fully readable; the effect is subtle by design at "Light" and "Medium", and more pronounced at "Heavy".

Is my file uploaded anywhere?

No — everything happens locally in your browser. Your PDF never leaves your device.
