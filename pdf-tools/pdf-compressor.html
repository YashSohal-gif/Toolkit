How it works

1. Choose your PDF

Drag and drop or click to select a PDF file.

2. Set your target size

Enter the exact size your form requires -- e.g. 100 KB, 200 KB, 1 MB.

3. Download

Each page is redrawn at a reduced quality until the whole PDF is as close as possible to your target size.

Note

This works best for PDFs that are mostly scanned pages/images (like signed forms). Text-heavy PDFs will be converted to image pages during compression.
