Tips for a clean scan

How do I get the best quality?

Use good lighting, keep the camera parallel to the page, and fill the frame with the document to avoid extra background.

What does the crop grid do?

Drag the four corner handles onto the page's edges before you capture — only the area inside the box is kept, so background clutter around the document gets cropped out automatically.

What does the scan filter do?

Auto Enhance stretches contrast so faded text reads darker and the background reads whiter — the classic "scanned document" look. B&W (High Contrast) pushes it further into pure black/white, great for text-only pages and smaller file sizes. Grayscale removes color without boosting contrast. The selected filter applies automatically to every page, whether captured by camera or uploaded.

Can I reorder or remove pages?

Yes — use the ↑/↓ buttons on each page thumbnail to reorder, or uncheck "Keep" to leave a page out before combining.

Is my photo uploaded anywhere?

No — the camera feed and every photo stay on your device. The PDF is built locally in your browser.
