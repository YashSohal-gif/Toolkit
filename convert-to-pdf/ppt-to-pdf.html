How it works

A .pptx file is really a zip of XML files. This reads the text out of each slide's XML and lays it out as a simple text page — useful for quickly getting speaker notes or slide text into a document, not for preserving your deck's design.

Is my file uploaded anywhere?

No — everything happens locally in your browser. Your presentation never leaves your device.
