How it works

Your HTML is rendered off-screen in your own browser and captured page-by-page into a PDF — nothing is sent to a server.

Can I convert a live website URL?

Not directly — since everything runs in your browser with no server, we can't fetch another website's page for you (that would require a server-side request). Save the page as an .html file first (most browsers: right-click → "Save As" → Webpage, HTML only), then upload it here.
