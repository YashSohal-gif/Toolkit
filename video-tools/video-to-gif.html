Tips for good GIFs

How long a clip can I convert?

Shorter clips (2-6 seconds) work best — GIF file sizes grow quickly with duration, frame rate, and width.

Why is my GIF choppy?

Try a lower frame rate or smaller width — very fast motion at low frame rates can look jumpy, so 12-15 fps is a good starting point.

Is my video uploaded anywhere?

No — everything happens locally in your browser using canvas frame capture. Your video never leaves your device.
