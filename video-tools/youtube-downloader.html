How to download YouTube videos?

1. Copy the link

Go to YouTube, find the video you want, and copy its URL from the address bar or the "Share" button.

2. Paste & Select Format

Paste the link into the box above. Choose whether you want an MP4 video file or an MP3 audio file.

3. Download

Click "Get Download Links" and choose your preferred quality (e.g. 720p, 1080p, or 320kbps MP3) to start downloading.
