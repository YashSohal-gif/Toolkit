Why this site?

Everything in one place

Compress a photo, make a passport photo, merge PDFs, convert Word to PDF, generate a QR code — no need to bounce between five different websites.

Is my photo or PDF uploaded anywhere?

No. Almost everything happens locally in your browser using JavaScript — your file never leaves your device.

Is it free?

Yes, completely free, no sign-up required. Larger PDF files show a short ad to keep the tools free — see each tool page for details.
