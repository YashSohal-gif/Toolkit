Voice to Text Exclusive

Dictate into your microphone and watch accurate text appear live — powered by your browser's built-in speech recognition.

This uses your browser's native speech recognition (best support in Chrome/Edge). Audio is processed by your browser directly — nothing is stored or uploaded by this site.

Tips for accurate dictation

Why isn't it working in my browser?

Live speech recognition is a browser feature — it works best in Chrome or Edge. Firefox and Safari have limited or no support for this API.

Does this work offline?

No — your browser sends short audio snippets to its own speech recognition service to transcribe (this is a browser feature, not something this site controls), so an internet connection is needed.

Can I edit the text afterward?

Yes — the textarea is fully editable, so you can clean up punctuation or fix any misheard words before copying or downloading.
