About PDF read-aloud

Which voices are available?

Whatever voices your browser/OS provides — this varies by device. Chrome, Edge, and Safari all include a set of system voices.

Can I jump to a specific part?

The currently-read sentence is highlighted in the text preview below the controls, so you can follow along as it reads.

Is my file uploaded anywhere?

No — everything happens locally in your browser. Your PDF never leaves your device.
