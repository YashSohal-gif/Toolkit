YouTube Thumbnail Downloader

Paste any YouTube video link and grab its thumbnail image in HD — free, instant, no sign-up.

This tool only fetches the public thumbnail image that YouTube itself serves for every video (the same image you see on the video preview). It does not download or convert the video or audio.

How it works

Where does the image come from?

YouTube automatically generates several thumbnail sizes for every public video (max resolution, high quality, medium, and default). This tool builds the direct image link from the video ID and shows you every available size to download.

Why do some sizes look blurry or missing?

Not every video has a true HD (maxresdefault) thumbnail uploaded — for older or lower-resolution videos, YouTube may not have generated one, so it falls back to a smaller size.
