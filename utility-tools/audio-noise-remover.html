How this works

What is spectral noise gating?

The audio is broken into short overlapping time windows, each analyzed in the frequency domain. The noise "fingerprint" you selected is subtracted from every window, then the cleaned windows are stitched back into audio.

Why do I need to select a quiet section?

The tool needs a short sample of just the background noise (no speech or music) to know what pattern to remove — pick 1-2 seconds of pure room tone or hiss.

Is my audio uploaded anywhere?

No — everything happens locally in your browser using the Web Audio API. Your file never leaves your device.
