Tips for IBPS PO photo & signature upload

Photo

Use a recent passport-style photo with a plain light background. Set the target size to match what your current notification asks for.

Signature

Sign on plain white paper with black or blue ink, photograph or scan it, then use this tool with a lower target size since signatures are usually smaller images.
