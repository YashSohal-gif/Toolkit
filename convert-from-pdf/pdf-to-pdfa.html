How it works

Each page is rendered as a flattened image (via pdf.js) and rebuilt into a new PDF (via jsPDF) with basic archival metadata set — no fonts, scripts, or external references to break in the future.

Why would I want this?

Flattened, image-based PDFs are less likely to look different when opened years from now on different software, since there's nothing but pixels to render — no fonts that might be missing, no interactive elements.

Is my file uploaded anywhere?

No — everything happens locally. Your PDF never leaves your device.
