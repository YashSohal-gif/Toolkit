How it works

Each page's text is read directly from the PDF (via pdf.js) and written into a real, valid .docx file that opens in Word, Google Docs, or LibreOffice — entirely in your browser.

Is my file uploaded anywhere?

No — everything happens locally. Your PDF never leaves your device.

What about scanned PDFs (images of text)?

If your PDF is a scanned image with no selectable text layer, there is no text to extract — this tool works on PDFs that already contain real, selectable text.
