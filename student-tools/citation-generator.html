Notes

Which style should I use?

APA is common in social sciences and education, MLA in humanities and literature, and Chicago in history and some publishing contexts. Check what your instructor or institution requires.

Is this a substitute for my institution's exact formatting rules?

This covers the most common citation cases correctly, but always double-check against your course's style guide for edge cases (multiple authors, no author, republished works, etc.).
