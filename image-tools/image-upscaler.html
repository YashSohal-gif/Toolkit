About upscaling

How is this different from an AI upscaler?

An AI super-resolution model tries to guess plausible new detail that wasn't in the original photo. This tool doesn't guess — it resizes using smooth, high-quality interpolation done in incremental steps, which looks noticeably cleaner than a single big stretch, but adds no new detail.

Will this fix a blurry or pixelated photo?

No — it makes the image bigger while keeping edges as clean as possible, but any existing blur or pixelation will scale up along with it.

Is my photo uploaded anywhere?

No — everything happens locally in your browser. Your photo never leaves your device.
