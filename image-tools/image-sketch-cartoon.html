How this works

How is the sketch made?

The photo is converted to grayscale, edges are detected using a Sobel filter, then inverted and blended to create a pencil-line look.

How is the cartoon effect made?

Colors are flattened into fewer bands (posterize) and dark edge lines are overlaid on top, mimicking a hand-drawn cartoon/comic look.

Is my photo uploaded anywhere?

No — everything happens locally in your browser. Your photo never leaves your device.
