How it works

1. Choose your file

Drag and drop or click to select a JPG or PNG photo.

2. Set your target size

Enter the exact size your form requires — e.g. 20 KB, 50 KB, 100 KB.

3. Download

The tool reduces quality and dimensions step by step until it's as close as possible to your target, then gives you a download link.

Lossy vs. Lossless — which should I pick?

Lossy (JPG, WebP) throws away detail the eye barely notices to shrink the file a lot — right for photos and any target-size goal. Lossless (PNG) keeps every pixel byte-for-byte identical to the resized original, so nothing is ever degraded — right for screenshots, logos, or graphics with sharp edges/text, at the cost of a bigger file. Because lossless has no quality dial, hitting a very small target size in PNG mode relies entirely on shrinking dimensions, not on visible quality loss.

Can I compress more than one image at once?

Yes — add multiple images and they're all compressed to the same target size with the same settings, then bundled into a single ZIP to download. Add just one image and it downloads directly, no ZIP step needed.
