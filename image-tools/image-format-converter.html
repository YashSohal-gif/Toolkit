How it works

Why can't I open HEIC photos from my iPhone?

iPhones save photos in HEIC format by default, which most non-Apple apps and websites can't open. This tool decodes HEIC right in your browser and converts it to a universally supported format.

Is my file uploaded anywhere?

No — everything happens locally in your browser. Your photo never leaves your device.
