How it works

The drawn, typed, or uploaded signature is exported as a PNG with a transparent background, so it drops cleanly onto any form, contract, or PDF.

I have an old signature photo — can I reuse it?

Yes — choose "Upload old signature", pick the photo or scan, and turn on "Remove white background" to make the paper transparent, leaving just the ink.

Is my signature uploaded anywhere?

No — everything happens locally in your browser. Nothing is ever sent to a server.
