How it works

Choosing a size

Pick a preset that matches your form's requirement, or enter a custom size in millimeters. Most Indian government forms use 3.5×4.5 cm.

Framing your photo

Drag inside the frame to reposition, and use the zoom slider to get closer or further from your face. What you see in the frame is exactly what gets exported.

Print sheet

The "Download 4×6 Print Sheet" button tiles several copies of your cropped photo onto a single 6×4 inch sheet at 300 DPI — hand it to any photo printing shop or home photo printer.

Is my photo uploaded anywhere?

No — everything happens locally in your browser. Your photo never leaves your device.
